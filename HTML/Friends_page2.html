Dunbar's Number




This number is the theoretical number of relationships of different levels of investment one can have. Tribe denotes that you know of them and they know of you. Clan denotes the group that you can classify as being a comrade with. example: Mg, Rb, K, Li, Sr , etc. Super Family denotes the group that you could call family example: close friendships, family, etc. Close Friends denotes a very close bond, one often forged through years or through a big event. example: Lovers, Lifelong Friends, etc. Here is a link to the wiki explaining it in a more scientific manner.
